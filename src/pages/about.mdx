Hi, I'm Connor — from Vancouver, BC, now building **Capso**.

For twenty years messengers have treated text as the product and everything else — content, actions, the small interactions that make a conversation alive — as attachments bolted on. Capso flips that. What you share and what you do are first-class inside the conversation. It's messaging redesigned around how friends actually relate to each other in 2026, not what fit into a 2010 push notification.

If you're building something adjacent, investing in consumer software, or just curious how the pieces fit together — I'd love to chat.
